About Us

Our Mission

"Find What’s Lost, Return What’s Found – Building a Responsible Campus Together!"

DDU Lost and found

Our motive is to provide a reliable platform for students and faculty to report and recover lost items within the college. Losing essential belongings can be frustrating, and our website simplifies the process by allowing users to report lost and found items quickly. This initiative fosters a culture of honesty, responsibility, and cooperation among students and staff. By making lost item recovery more efficient, we aim to reduce stress and encourage a supportive community. Our platform ensures transparency, accessibility, and convenience, ultimately creating a safer and more connected environment where everyone can help each other.

Our team of award-winning designers and developers share a passion for perfection.

Our Principlees

1

Honesty & Integrity

Encouraging users to report and return lost items truthfully, fostering a trustworthy community.

2

Efficiency & Accessibility

Providing a simple and user-friendly platform where lost and found items can be reported and retrieved quickly.

3

Community & Responsibility

Promoting a culture of accountability, cooperation, and mutual assistance within the college.
